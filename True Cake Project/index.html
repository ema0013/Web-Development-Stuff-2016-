About us
Edwin Ma and Bryant Hua are two friends who grew up in Brooklyn, New York and have always had an interest in baking. The two great friends moved to South El Monte, California in 2000 looking to take over South El Monte by storm. They bake all kinds of cake ranging from the regular Cheesecake to cakes for special occasions.

Customer Favorites!!!
